Academics at Sta. Lucia High School

Sta. Lucia High School offers a comprehensive and dynamic academic program that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. Our curriculum is designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students, and it emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration.

Core Subjects

Our core academic subjects include:

  • Mathematics
  • Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • English Language Arts
  • Social Studies (History, Geography, Economics)
  • Filipino

Electives and Special Programs

In addition to our core subjects, we offer a variety of electives and special programs to enhance the educational experience of our students:

  • Computer Science and Information Technology
  • Arts and Music
  • Physical Education and Health
  • Foreign Languages
  • Advanced Placement (AP) Courses

Extracurricular Activities

Sta. Lucia High School believes in the importance of extracurricular activities in developing well-rounded individuals. Our students have the opportunity to participate in a wide range of activities, including:

  • Sports Teams (Basketball, Volleyball, Soccer, etc.)
  • Student Government and Leadership Programs
  • Clubs and Organizations (Science Club, Art Club, etc.)
  • Community Service Projects

Support Services

We are committed to supporting the academic and personal growth of our students. Our support services include:

  • Guidance and Counseling
  • Special Education Programs
  • Tutoring and Academic Support
  • College and Career Counseling
